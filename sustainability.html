Sustainable Development Goals

Our actions are motivated by Gaia Capitalism; a principle that combines the financial and humanitarian aspects of our shared world. Our belief is that every organisation needs to play a role in making a positive change in the world. Manutratech Industries is committed to being a part of that change through the Clean Haven brand. We believe that the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals can be achieved if we are intentional about them and if we all played a part to ensure they are achieved in a sustainable manner. We are working to craft a framework that can be able to fully address the United Nations Sustainable Goals and adopted world over by the year 2029. We are however already focused on the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals in the following ways primarily:

Clean Water & Sanitation

We are currently involved in ensuring that sanitation is observed by making our products more and more accessible to people from all walks of life, as we also work towards forming partnerships with organisations that turn dirty water into clean water that is suitable for human consumption. We are also working to start water harvesting, which few have been able to successfully, as so much water goes into the drain and is wasted during the rainy season in Africa.

Climate Action

Climate change is becoming a reality to almost everyone who at one point may have considered it a myth. This is being proven as time passes through the signs of resistance being shown by the Earth as various earthquakes, typhoons, hurricanes, and floods are being witnessed across the world. With greenhouse gas emissions at an all-time high and vegetation cover lessening with industrialisation advances, the time to act is now as the situation is only worsening. Our simplistic approach to Climate Action involves trying to reset our world to the way it was before it became one big concrete jungle, but at the same time adjusting ourselves to the modern day requirements.

Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure

As an Industrial & Innovative company, it is our mission to further industrialize our continent and impact the future generations to continue with industry and innovation to be competitive in a fast paced and dynamic world. It is within our vision to have an Industrial & Innovation Academy that is able to realize the hidden potential of people that may never be realized if not harnessed in the right way.

Responsible Consumption and Production

A principle that we apply in production is using only what is necessary to avoid any wastage and maximize on output. The same principle needs to be applied when it comes to consumption. Recycling of products ensures that the earth is sustainable and that we do not have to overproduce which has a ripple effect to the disposal of the same. We are soon going to offer product discounts to clients when empty plastic bottles of Clean Haven products are returned to selected retail stores to be launched in already existing stores near you.
