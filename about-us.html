About Us.

Who We Are?

Manutratech Industries is a Fast Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) manufacturing company based in Kenya established on the 12th of January 2019. Our products trade under the Clean Haven brand which has grown to be a very trusted brand in households in Nairobi and its environs in our years of operation. Our company is its people and just like the lion, we are fearless, bold and work as a team to deliver the highest quality products for our customers. Manutratech Industries, through the Clean Haven brand is aligned to doing Global Good. This is through our Sustainable Development Goals which we are crafting and developing to be able to roll out to our consumers and partners. Our policies are our guiding tower and they enable us to always stay deeply rooted to our beliefs and principles.

Mission

To be a manufacturing powerhouse in Kenya and become a household name.

Vision

To have our products in every home by 2030!

Our Values

We are lions and that's what separates us from the rest in the jungle. As the king of the jungle we have a role to play in setting the pace for the others. Our principles are our guiding tower:

  • Our company is its people.
  • We believe in being the best version of ourselves everyday and reflecting that in the work that we do.
  • The world around us was born out of imagination. When we let our imagination run wild and be limitless, we are capable of creating anything.
  • We put the needs of our customers first.
  • There are no shortcuts to any place worth going. We take out time to ensure we do the right thing and live within our means.
  • In everything that we do we are always crafting solutions to bring about sustainable change to our planet.
