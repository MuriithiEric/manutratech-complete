Environmental Policy

As a leading manufacturer of soap and detergents in Kenya, we are committed to ensuring that we leave the environment better than we found it. We have a zero tolerance towards careless disposal of our product containers and our 2029 sustainable consumption and development goals already includes a strategy to ensure that our products are disposed off responsibly to ensure that the environment does not suffer from effects caused by littering. Our company supports initiatives geared towards environmental sustainability and we are ready to partner with like minded organisations to achieve our common objectives.

Code of Business Conduct

In a society that embraces corruption as the status quo, Manutratech exists to prove that you can succeed by upholding high standards of integrity and ethics in all our business dealings. We have a zero tolerance towards corruption and for that reason, we work to ensure that we are in good standing with the laws of the land that we operate in and that we plan to set up base in the future.

Human Rights Policy

As we continuously strive to be a market leader and make the world a better place, we are intentional about ensuring that we uphold high standards of integrity and principles that do not violate human rights of any individual. Our Human Rights policies ensures that every member of our business community is treated with dignity. Our policies cut across the freedom of association and movement, no child labour or forced labor for any individual, working compensation, security of workers and a conducive working environment.

Product Quality

Our goal is to ensure that Clean Haven products achieve 99% consumer satisfaction. We believe that the key to this is continuous improvement (kaizen) and being customer centric. Our very capable team of engineers work extremely hard to ensure that product quality and standards are maintained during every batch of production.

Anti-Harassment and Discrimination Policy

We believe that every human life is valuable and that the work culture can greatly affect the households in which our team come from. This has a ripple effect to the upbringing of a healthy society as it trickles down to how friends, lovers, wives, husbands, and children are treated as it directly or indirectly affects the people that they interact with. We are committed to ensuring an inclusive and safe space for every individual regardless of their race, gender, sexual orientation, tribe, culture, religious beliefs, age, political affiliations and ideologies. This is achieved by mutual respect and an understanding that we all come from very diverse backgrounds.
