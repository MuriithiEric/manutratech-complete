Clean Haven Liquid Soap Hacks That You Wish You Knew

MARCH 18, 2021

BY MICHELLE JEMATOR

It’s time to rest if you’ve been looking for economical ways to stretch your liquid soap and make it last longer to save you some coins. Fortunately, some of my tips also come in handy to make your soap last longer when store shelves are empty.

If you are using a liquid soap with a pump cap on it, you know the amount that each press on the pump releases. I believe you can make your liquid soap last longer if you allow less to come out. However, this depends on the quality of soap. Is it foamy enough? Is it gel-like or more watery? You have to take into account all these factors. All in all, this trick still stretches your liquid soap. I’m sure you don’t need two pumps to wash your hands. One pump will still foam evenly (depending on the quality).

When it comes to dish soap, I don’t think people do enough to save up. You don’t need a squirt for every dish. That sponge still has more soap. This means that you’re wasting soap if you have to squirt a bit for every dish. Now, imagine how much soap goes down that drain. What a waste! Here is a free tip: put some water in a bowl or in the sink secured with a stopper, or in a small kitchen basin enough to accommodate the dishes you need to clean. Put some Clean Haven liquid soap in it and start washing the dishes. You should only add more when the bubbles decrease or when it’s no longer cutting grease. This way, you even get to save on water. You have to think like a conservationist when you want so save up on supplies.

Anyone's life, truly well lived consists of work, sunshine, exercise, soap, plenty of fresh air, and a happy contented spirit.
Adding just 100 ml of water to every 500 ml of Clean haven Liquid Soap ensures that it lasts longer.
Another brilliant way is to add 500 ml to every 500 ml of Clean Haven liquid soap. This will surely stretch it out. Be careful not to make it to dilute as you will tamper with the quality of the liquid soap. When you have that conservationist mindset, you’ll learn how to conserve anything be it water, electricity, household supplies, work supplies, energy, and even the environment which is basically your habitat.
